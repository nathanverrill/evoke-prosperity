1. Keel
   Keel is a desolate, non-developed town, so its residents rely on hard labor in order to
   earn their daily wages and survive.
   Mining Minigame:

Players are instructed by B1llBot to head to the mines. Here, they can progress by
mining the required ores and blocks. With the wages players earn, they can choose to
purchase better pickaxes to aid their mining tasks, or save up to purchase a train ticket
that will allow them to move into Halyard.
Secret Parkour:

There is a secret parkour game hidden within the streets of Keel. By completing this
course, players are granted a special prize that will also aid in their progression of the
server. 2. Halyard
Halyard is a more progressive civilization compared to Keel, there are two methods of earning
currency. Additionally, these two methods require more strategy and thinking to complete tasks
rather than straight labor
Factory Crafting Minigame:

In this minigame, players collect resources from conveyor belts. These items are not
guaranteed, and players must think ahead to decide which items to craft. Difficult
recipes reward more currency compared to easier ones. Players are also able to obtain
resources through other, more indirect, methods if needed (i.e. collecting eggs from
chickens). The goal of this minigame is to teach students how to plan ahead, how to be
resourceful, and the importance of hard work.
Mob Arena:

Players must fight waves of mobs that progressively get harder. With the drops earned from
killing mobs, they are able to sell them to a shop to gain currency. Players are then able to
choose to save for a ticket to the Oasis or spend this currency on upgrading tools. As mob
waves increase in difficulty, drop rates for artifacts also increase to reward players on their hard
work.

3. Oasis
   Once players reach the Oasis, they are given a plot of land in which they can freely build with
   blocks and resources gained through minigames or missions.
