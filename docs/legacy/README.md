# Legacy — not canon

Everything in this folder is superseded draft material, kept only for historical context.

The current canon lives in [`../canon/`](../canon/README.md) — specifically the five infographics and `stakeholderfeedback.md`.

## Why this is legacy

`All NPC and World Prompts/` was built around a version of the world where:

- Alpha Dynamics never withdrew from the mountain — it still actively runs Keel as an extractive company town (coal mining, rent traps, a casino designed to be a losing strategy).
- Alex is secretly Alpha's former CEO, who fell from the Oasis to Keel and lost his memory. Billbot's dialogue rules are built around hiding and eventually revealing this twist.
- There is no "Brokers" faction filling a post-Alpha power vacuum.

Current canon instead has Alpha Dynamics withdraw after a lithium market crash, a faction called "the Brokers" seize control of water access, and Alex arrive as an outside builder (an "EVOKE graduate") with no CEO backstory. If the team ever wants to revive elements from this legacy draft (the NPC roster, the zone-based teaching-focus structure, the minigame mechanics), reconcile them against current canon first rather than treating this folder as ground truth.
